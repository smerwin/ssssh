# Privacy Policy

ssssh doesn't collect, transmit, or sell any personal data. Specifically:

- **No analytics, no tracking, no accounts.** ssssh doesn't talk to any
  server operated by its developer. There's nothing to opt out of.
- **Everything you enter stays on your device.** Host profiles, key
  metadata, and known-host fingerprints are stored locally in your
  device's Application Support directory. Private key material is stored
  in the iOS Keychain, gated behind Face ID/Touch ID or your device
  passcode, and never leaves it.
- **SSH connections go only where you tell them to.** When you connect to
  a host you've configured, your credentials and terminal session go
  directly to that host over SSH -- not through any server operated by
  ssssh's developer.
- **Purchases are handled entirely by Apple.** In-app purchases and
  subscriptions are processed by StoreKit; ssssh never sees your payment
  details, only whether a purchase succeeded.

If this policy ever changes, the update will be reflected in this file in
the app's [GitHub repository](https://github.com/smerwin/ssssh).
